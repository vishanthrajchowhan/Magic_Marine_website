Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common service questions

How often should I schedule maintenance on my vessel?

+

The frequency of maintenance depends on your vessel's age, usage, and manufacturer recommendations. Generally, we recommend seasonal maintenance and annual inspections. We can create a customized maintenance schedule after assessing your specific vessel.

What types of vessels do you service?

+

We service all types of vessels including luxury yachts, sailboats, motorboats, fishing vessels, and commercial fleet boats. Our technicians have experience with vessels ranging from 20 feet to over 150 feet in length.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

+

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency repair services. Contact us immediately if your vessel requires urgent attention. Our rapid response team will assist you as quickly as possible to minimize downtime.

What is included in a vessel inspection?

+

Our comprehensive inspection includes engine diagnostics, hull and structural assessment, electrical systems check, plumbing and sanitation review, safety equipment verification, and detailed condition reporting with recommendations.

Are your technicians certified and insured?

+

All our technicians are certified marine professionals with extensive industry experience. Magic Marine Service carries comprehensive liability insurance covering all work performed on your vessel.

How do I get a service quote?

+

Simply contact us through our website or call us directly. We'll review your vessel's needs and provide a recommended plan of action with clear next steps.
